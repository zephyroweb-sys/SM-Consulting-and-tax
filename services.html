COMPREHENSIVE SERVICES

Expert handling of accounting, tax strategies, and regulatory compliance to safeguard your business.

Accounting & Payroll

Accurate, transparent, and compliant accounting lays the groundwork for all financial growth. Our dedicated teams ensure your baseline numbers are spotless.

  • Bookkeeping: Daily data entry, ledger maintenance, and bank reconciliations.
  • Financial Reporting: Preparation of Profit & Loss, Balance Sheets, and Cash Flow assertions.
  • Payroll Processing: Accurate salary calculation, payroll compliance, and distribution.

Taxation & Compliance

We navigate complex Indian tax frameworks to minimize your liability while ensuring 100% adherence to deadlines and policies.

  • Income Tax Filing: Holistic review and submissions for individuals and corporations.
  • GST Services: Registrations, monthly/quarterly filings, and annual audits.
  • TDS/TCS: Deduction guidelines and quarterly compliance.
  • STPI & FLA: Advanced filings for specifically positioned IT/ITES organizations.

Statutory Registrations

Setting up your business involves maneuvering through varied government portals. We make it seamless.

  • Business Incorporation: Private Limited, LLP, Partnership Firm setups.
  • PAN & TAN: Prompt applications and status tracking.
  • Labor Law Compliance: PF (Provident Fund) & ESI setups.
  • Additional Certifications: MSME (Udyam), Food License (FSSAI), Trade Licenses.

Advisory & Corporate Support

Beyond standard filings, we operate as strategic partners guiding you through multifaceted acquisitions, restructuring, and systemic optimization.

  • Tax Planning: Proactive identification of tax-saving avenues within the legal framework.
  • Legal Consultancy: Contract reviewing and basic legal advisory.
  • International Compliance: Navigating foreign remittances and cross-border regulatory measures (FEMA).

To be a trusted and leading accounting, tax, and business advisory firm in India, driving sustainable growth for our clients through unwavering integrity.
