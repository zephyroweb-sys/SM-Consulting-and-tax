WHO WE ARE

Driven by ethical principles, we deliver value-driven financial compliance and consulting to empower businesses.

SM Accounting and Tax Consultants is a professionally managed firm providing comprehensive services in Auditing, Accounting, Taxation, Business Advisory, and Financial Planning. Established in 2015, the firm has grown into a trusted partner for all businesses & professionals across PAN India.

With over 11 years of industry experience, we deliver reliable, ethical, and value-driven financial and compliance solutions. Our approach focuses on simplifying complex regulatory requirements and providing clients with practical guidance that supports informed business decisions and sustainable growth.

Over the years, we have built strong and lasting relationships with our clients, proudly serving 500+ companies and thousands of individuals across diverse industries. Our reputation is built on professional integrity, timely execution, and personalized client support.

Our core service offerings include Accounting & Payroll Management, Income Tax Compliance, GST Filing & Advisory, TDS Compliance, STPI Compliance, Statutory Registrations, ROC Compliance, and Corporate Accounting Support for both domestic and international clients.

At SM Accounting and Tax Consultants, we are committed to delivering accurate, efficient, and secure financial services in time enabling our clients to remain fully compliant while focusing on their core business operations.
